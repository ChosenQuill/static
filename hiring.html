Need:

I need graphic designers, and app designers (web designers could also do the task) for logos, app designs, game assets, and cool art. I want to find people who can work with me in the long term but even a small contribution or helping for a short amount of time would be amazing. This project is very large so you will most likely have something you want to do.

As I said before, I AM NOT LOOKING FOR MONEY. Any money I earn whenever will be given to the person who helped me with that project. Most of my projects will be based on being free for the people who want to use it. (If I made everything I did paid, how can I give back to the community?)

You choose what you want to do and how long you want to do it. Any help I can get is greatly valued. Even if you want to just create a logo over the timespan of 2 months, I would be happy that you are there to help.

If you would like to help me, please follow the contacts at the bottom of the page. I will reply as soon as possible.
